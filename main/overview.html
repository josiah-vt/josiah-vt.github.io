Overview of internet privacy

If you've only recently become interested in internet privacy, you may enjoy examining some of the below materials. They'll acquaint you with a few issues associated with internet privacy and remind you what you might want to keep in mind when deciding what aspects of internet privacy you value and want to protect.

Glenn Greenwald: Why privacy matters

This is a presentation by Glenn Greenwald, a prominent journalist and proponent of internet privacy, on internet privacy and its importance. It's a good introduction to some of the most frequently-discussed issues related to internet privacy.

privacyrights.org

privacyrights.org is a website dedicated to helping individuals protect their privacy. This guide provides a thorough introduction to threats to readers' online privacy and what they can do to maintain their privacy. It clearly explains many different terms and is welcoming to those who aren't already highly knowledgable about technology.

Short video--introduction to privacy resources

This is a brief video that provides an introduction to some privacy resources, including some of the ones above. If you want a one-minute overview of helpful resources for individuals interested in internet privacy, you might find this helpful.
